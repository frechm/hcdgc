Woodruff Leisure Center

The Woodruff Leisure Center DGC was established in 2017 and designed by Innova's Russell Schwarz. The course has seen slight alterations to accommodate additional baskets (holes 3/5) and a shorter red tee layout for ladies, juniors, and less experienced players. The course utilizes Innova DISCatcher Pro28 targets.

The Woodruff DGC (5,170 ft.) is a heavily wooded course with many holes featuring OB from the creek that winds through the course. Many holes feature an elevation change, including the signature hole 11, which flows 369 ft. from the top of a hill down to a beautiful, raised basket with brick features. The leads directly to one of Hub City's most difficult holes: the tightly wooded hole 12 (153 ft.) with an OB creek down to the left.
