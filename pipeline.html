The Pipeline DGC (6,289 ft.) was established in 2015 and designed by Innova's Russell Schwarz. The course has seen slight alterations to accommodate additional pin placements and baskets, as well as a shorter, red tee layout for ladies, juniors, and less experienced players. The course utilizes Innova DISCatcher Pro28 targets.

Pipeline is a heavily wooded courses with numerous elevation changes. Hole 11 is the signature hole, which features a 399 ft. downhill shot to a slightly raised basket on a green guarded by large trees and an OB creek.
