The Moo

The Moo DGC (5,200 ft.), aptly named for its location in Cowpens, SC, was established in 2020 and designed by Innova's Russell Schwarz. The course features the unique layout of 10 tee pads and 18 baskets. The course utilizes Innova DISCatcher Pro28 targets.

While it is relatively short in length (2,500 ft. for 9 holes), the Moo has a lot of character. The course features numerous elevation changes, heavily wooded fairways, and OB creeks. This includes the shared fairway of holes 5 (297 ft.) and 14 (543 ft.), which features a treacherous creek to the right side of the fairway.
