Linville Hills Park

The Linville Hills DGC was established in 2020 and designed by Vern Weygandt, Spartanburg Park's Jonathan Smith, and Innova's Russel Schwarz. Designed for beginner and casual players, this 1,215 ft. course is an official Innova “Ace Place.” The course utilizes Innova DISCatcher Pro28 targets.

The Linville Hills DGC features slight elevation changes and OB walking paths. It also includes two raised baskets on holes 4 and 8.
