The Holston Creek DGC (10,285 ft.) was established in 2016 and designed by Innova's Russell Schwarz. The course has seen slight alterations to accommodate additional short tees (holes 18/26). The course utilizes Innova DISCatcher Pro28 targets.

The Holston Creek DGC is a 27-hole championship level course. It features 18 holes on moderately wooded property followed by 9 longer open holes. Three of the holes include water carries over a small pond and there are numerous elevation changes throughout the course. Hole 9 (360 ft. uphill) is the signature hole, which features the now famous hanging basket suspended from a giant mailbox.
